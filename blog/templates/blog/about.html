Winner VivaTech hackathon. (May 2020 – May 2020)

Participated and won in the Vivatech hackathon in Paris. This hackathon consisted of two days and one night of coding and idea development. Our proposed solution was to a problem put forth by Eramet, a mining company. Eramet is very conscious about its social and environment impact and therefore requested the participants to come up with a method toincrease supply chain transparency. Our solution used graph theory and reinforcement learning principles in order to provide such a solution for Eramet.
